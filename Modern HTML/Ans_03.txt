Accessibility in Web Development:

Ensuring that websites are accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.
Importance of Accessible Websites

Accessible websites provide equal access and opportunities to people with various disabilities.
They are also important for legal compliance with standards like the ADA and WCAG.
Benefits for User Groups

Visual Impairments: Websites can be designed to be compatible with screen readers and offer alternative text for images.
Hearing Impairments: Providing captions and transcripts for audio and video content.
Motor Disabilities: Designing for keyboard-only navigation and voice recognition software.
Cognitive Disabilities: Using clear language and providing the ability to customize the user interface.
Elderly Users: Considering age-related changes in vision, hearing, and motor skills.
Mobile Users: Ensuring websites are responsive and adaptable to various screen sizes.
